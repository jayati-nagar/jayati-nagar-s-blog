The bad guys episode 1
A movie in a book

When I say,'a movie in a book' I dont exactly mean it, but it was kind of like an action movie, where the buddies a planning some strategies, and they go on their adventure kind of thing, it's so interestiong, that it creates a total image in your mind about it happening (well, at least for me)

Thea stilton:The journey to Atlantis
An adventure

It was kind of a detective book, and not related to sherlock holmes at all. It's about five freinds (Thea sisters) who are set on an adventure to get Atlas, a boy from Atlantis, who came to Earth. They find a lot of clues, and its a lot of fun!

Tom Gates: A tiny bit lucky
A simple life, yet so Interesting...

So, it's a simple life of Tom, who has a little bit of luck, which he inclueds with his doodles, caramel wafers and his very grumpy sister Delia, I olove this book because not only it has good doodles, but also it gives a hilarious comic vibe. It's Amazing!!
